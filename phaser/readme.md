Here are 5 easy mini project ideas you can build using Phaser:

1. **Catch the Falling Objects**  
Objects fall from the top of the screen, and the player controls a basket or character to catch them. Score increases for each object caught.

2. **Simple Platformer**  
Create a basic side-scrolling platformer where the player jumps between platforms, collects coins, and avoids obstacles.

3. **Memory Card Game**  
A grid of cards is displayed face down. Players flip two cards at a time to find matching pairs.

4. **Top-Down Shooter**  
The player controls a spaceship or character that can move in all directions and shoot enemies appearing from the edges.

5. **Breakout/Brick Breaker**  
A paddle at the bottom bounces a ball to break bricks at the top. The player must prevent the ball from falling off the screen.
